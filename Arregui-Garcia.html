Beatriz Arregui-García

master student

beatriz.arregui-garcia@iplesp.upmc.fr

Bio: Beatriz studied a Bachelor's degree in Physics at Universidad Complutense de Madrid (Spain) following the Fundamental Physics track. In 2017-18 she attended Università degli Studi di Trieste as an Erasmus exchange student and performed a three months internship at the ICTP, where she worked on stochastic models and interacting systems on finance. From September 2019 she is a student of the Physics of Complex Systems Master in Paris held by Sorbonne Université, Paris-Sud Saclay and Université de Paris.

Research activities: Beatriz main interests lie in the study of social and ecologic systems, from the complex networks and agent based modelling point of view.
