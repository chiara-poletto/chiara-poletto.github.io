bio

I received a PhD in physics at the University of Padova (Italy) in Mar 2009 with a thesis on physics of biopolymers entitled "Solvent induced interactions in biopolymers: origin of secondary motifs" conducted within the Laboratory of Interdisciplinary Physics. From Feb 2009 to December 2013 I was Post Doc at the Computational Epidemiology Laboratory, ISI Foundation, Torino (Italy). I moved to the Institut Pierre Louis d'Epidémiologie et de Santé Publique (iPLESP), INSERM UMR S 1136 in Paris (France)  in Oct 2012, first as a visitor researcher with a fellowship from the French Embassy in Italy and then as a Post Doc. Since Oct 2015 I am researcher (Chargé de Recherche) at iPLESP.
