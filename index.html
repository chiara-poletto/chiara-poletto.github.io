networks in disease ecology

network epidemiology, network physics, disease ecology, outbreak analysis

We are a research group at Pierre Louis Institute of Epidemiology and Public Health part of INSERM. We combin complex-system physics and heavy computation with epidemiology to study the drivers of pathogens' spread in human and animal populations.
